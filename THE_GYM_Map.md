# THE GYM — Platform Map

*Sister site to **The Dose**. Same engine, same cast model, same "verify before you believe" promise — re-skinned for fitness, supplements, smoothies, light workouts, yoga, and the reverse-aging hype machine.*

**Tagline:** *Verify before you believe.* (shared with The Dose)
**Sub-tagline option:** *The gym staff who read the studies, not the labels.*
**Flagship hook:** Reverse-aging, checked. The supplement aisle is 90% marketing — the Stoplight calls it.

---

## 1. The core idea (inherited from The Dose)

The Dose graduated its own **"Fitness, Supplements & Recovery"** page into a full platform. Everything that made The Dose work carries over:

- A **Stoplight** verification engine (red / yellow / green verdict + clickable citations) run by an MCP fact-checker.
- A **cast of domain personas**, each with a card (eyes-open / eyes-closed portrait), a profile page, rotating personal notes, and cross-team "floor" dynamics.
- **Audio**: a daily briefing podcast, guided meditation, and now guided workouts + yoga/breathwork.
- The **no-em-dash, plain-spoken** house voice and the "educational, not medical" disclaimer spine.
- **Characters travel** — the backpack (MCP) travels with them (per the design rule).

## 5. The verification engine & the cards

- **The Gym Stoplight** = the same red/yellow/green verdict + named-source citations as The Dose. Eli runs it; Henry, Nadia, Amara, and Claire carry their own backpacks so a verdict can cite live **even when Eli isn't in the room** (the design rule).
- **Cards** = the same system: eyes-open / eyes-closed portraits, a profile page that auto-lists every page the agent appears on (via a Gym `cast-affiliations.js`: host / partner / silent / teasing), and rotating `personal-notes` in each character's voice.
- **Floor dynamics to seed:** Reece teases Coach Dom for "boring" programming; Dr. Lena Brandt overrules Reece on anything clinical; Wyatt and Nadia argue about whether a smoothie is a meal or a dessert; Henry quietly debunks the TikTok Reece sends him.

## 6. Safety (do not skip)

Fitness + supplements + body image carries real risk, so The Gym needs the same care The Dose gives eating disorders:

- **Overtraining & Body Image** page: handled by Dr. Claire + Nadia, plain and non-sensational, with a route to professional support. Use the **National Alliance for Eating Disorders** helpline (not NEDA, which is disconnected).
- **Reverse-aging / longevity** claims and **stimulant pre-workouts / SARMs / peptides** route through the Stoplight + Henry + Claire, never hyped.
- **"Educational, not medical"** disclaimer spine carried verbatim from The Dose, plus FTC/FDA-safe framing on every supplement and anti-aging claim.
