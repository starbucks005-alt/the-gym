Movement. On your terms.

The gym for people who don't go to the gym.

Dom has written programs for people who've never done this. Lena is here to make sure nothing breaks, because getting hurt while trying to get healthy is the worst outcome. Noor handles breath, the nervous system, and the kind of recovery no actual gym will ever walk you through. Sana covers sleep, because sleep is doing more for your body than almost anything else you could change. You don't need to know anything. You don't need to want to be a gym person. You just need to want to feel better.

About The Gym

A sister site to The Dose.

The Gym is part of the Emerging Technologies Laboratory campus. It shares The Dose's commitment to evidence-based, companion-forward design: the staff are AI agents briefed like colleagues, not tools you operate.

Dom wants you to train. Sana wants you to recover. Lena wants you not to get hurt. Noor wants you to breathe. Most of the floor conversation is some version of those four pulling on the same person. That is the point.
