      How the staff verify (so you don't have to take our word for it)

      Every myth check on this page got run through The Gym Stoplight before it reached you. Eli pulls the studies, Coach Dom checks the training science, Henry flags anything that touches a supplement or a stimulant, and Dr. Claire calls it when a move belongs in a clinic, not a comment section.

      Green means the evidence backs it. Amber means "it depends, and here's on what." Red means the internet is lying to you again. Each verdict carries a named source you can look up yourself.
